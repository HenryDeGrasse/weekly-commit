## What I implemented

### New DTOs (`reconcile/dto/`)
- **`AddCommitData`** — fields for adding a commit post-lock (title, chessPiece, rcdoNodeId, etc.)
- **`EditCommitChanges`** — nullable patch for post-lock edits (estimatePoints, chessPiece, rcdoNodeId, priorityOrder)
- **`ScopeChangeRequest`** — single envelope for ADD/REMOVE/EDIT scope-change operations
- **`ScopeChangeEventResponse`** — read model for individual scope-change audit events

- **`ScopeChangeTimelineResponse`** — list of events + manager exception flags

- **`ReconciliationViewResponse`** — full reconciliation dashboard DTO
- **`SetOutcomeRequest`** — outcome + required notes for PUT /outcome

### Services
- **`ScopeChangeService`**: `addPostLockCommit`, `removePostLockCommit` (sets outcome=CANCELED), `editPostLockCommit` (one event per field with before/after), `getChangeTimeline`, `detectManagerExceptions` (King-change and >20% points increase rules)
- **`ReconciliationService`**: `openReconciliation` (idempotent LOCKED→RECONCILING + auto-achieve DONE tickets), `getReconciliationView`, `setCommitOutcome` (notes required for PA/NA/CANCELED), `submitReconciliation` (validates all outcomes, creates immutable snapshots, transitions to RECONCILED)
- **`AutoReconcileJob`**: Scheduled job (every minute) that opens reconciliation for LOCKED plans past their reconcile deadline
